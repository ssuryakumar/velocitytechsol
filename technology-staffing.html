our services

“IT Staffing” has increasingly become a business consisting of mostly “limited value” staffing agencies more worried about margins and headcount than with quality of service. These firms usually have high employee turnover (often the rate of turnover is directly proportional to the size of the firm). This coupled with their inexperienced staff who usually have no technology backgrounds produces a very unsatisfactory long term experience for companies seeking good talent and a partner who can support their needs consistently. VTS has the goal to change the nature of this relationship and raise the bar. We feel that clients are seeking and deserve the “next level” of IT Staffing support. This starts with a long term commitment to working with our clients to understand their organization and business model so that we can properly match the right candidates (Consultant or Full Time). VTS recognizes that companies grow and evolve and that their technology and consulting needs continue to change. Talent is our business. Networking and Verticalization are the keys to our success. By understanding the best talent available within key verticals and keeping an active network of connections within that vertical means that talent comes to VTS seeking the best client opportunities that will help them with their next career step. Our commitment to our consultants does not end with their placement. We help manage their careers and provide additional training, networking, and resources as necessary to keep their attention focused on our client’s projects. Experience. Ongoing personal education. Active professional networking. Honest business practices. Technology backgrounds. Access to top talent. Treating consultants with respect. These are the cornerstones of our business.
