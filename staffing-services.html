STAFFING SERVICES

Technology Staffing has increasingly become a business consisting of agencies more worried about margins and headcount than with quality of service.

VTS has the goal to change the nature of this relationship and raise the bar. We feel that clients are seeking and deserve the “next level” of IT Staffing support.

This starts with a long-term commitment to working with our clients to understand their organization and business model so that we can properly match the right candidates (Consultant or Full Time). VTS recognizes that companies grow and evolve and that their Technology and Consulting needs continue to change.

Talent is our business. Networking, Verticalization and Education are the keys to our success. By understanding the best talent available within key Verticals and keeping an active Network of connections within that vertical means that talent comes to VTS seeking the best client opportunities that will help them with their next career step.

Education means that we continue to train ourselves to understand the latest technology trends and constantly refine our methods for screening and identifying candidates to determine their true capabilities.

In addition, our commitment to our Consultants does not end with their placement. We help manage their careers and provide additional training, networking, and resources as necessary to keep their attention focused on our clients’ projects.

Experience. Ongoing personal education. Active professional networking. Honest business practices. Identifying top talent. Treating clients and consultants with respect. These are the cornerstones of our business.



VTS specializes in the Recruiting of Technology and Business talent including (but not limited to)

  • UI/UX Developers
  • Mobile Application Developers (iOS, Android)
  • Big Data resources (Hadoop)
  • Web and Client-Server Application Developers
  • Database Developers and Administrators
  • Project Managers
  • Business Analysts
  • Data Analysts
  • Systems Administrators
  • Helpdesk/Desktop Support
  • Product Managers
  • Quality Assurance (QA) Analysts and Testers
  • ERP Professionals (SAP primarily)
  • Business Intelligence consultants
